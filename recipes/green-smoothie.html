Green

SERVES ONE

5 mins
  • Vegan
  • Gluten-Free

  • 1/4 an Avocado
  • 1 Ripe Pear
  • 1/2 an Apple
  • 1 Handful of Baby Greens
  • 1/3 Lemon Juice
  • 12 Almonds
  • 2 tbsp Superfood Mix
  • 3 Cups of Water
  • 1 Handful of Ice

Smoothies are super easy, anyone can make them and that's only one of the reasons why we just love them. Simply chop, blend, and drink. Wether you enjoy yours at breakfast, lunch, or dinner (or even as a snack) you can be sure that you're getting the nutrients and vitamins that your body needs.
