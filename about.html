About Us


Charlie and Joe

Joe is from southern Indiana, and I (Charlie) am from North Yorkshire in the United Kingdom - we met in London. Somewhat randomly, we started Roamers when we moved to Indianapolis, Indiana, after several years in the corporate world in London, and a 9 month trip across North America and parts of Asia and Australia. We both now work in asset management for large non-profit organizations here in Indianapolis.

When we first dreamed up Roamers (mostly in our chilled-out-work-free-zen-adventure mindset), we thought we would be a cool plantbased caf√© that specialized in smoothies and brunch and would take Indiana by storm! Once we did some research, we realized we stood no chance in the catering world. So we stuck to what we knew best (our careers to date) yet found more purposeful roles, and still fit in a healthy lifestyle with wholesome eating habits, including our daily smoothie for breakfast with fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, etc. and so Roamers Superfood Mix was born.

We got married in 2019. We like to hang out with friends, cook, make home improvements and just started fostering rescue dogs! Joe gets to play soccer 3 times a week (IU Soccer grad) and I have found my workout jam (and my abs!) through barre classes. We are still passionate about travel.
