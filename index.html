Join us on our journey to health and wellness

We brought the Roamers Superfood Mix to the market to share with you an aspect of how we fit in a nutrient rich diet on a busy schedule.

The ingredients are a wholefood blend of organic seeds, organic oats and non-GMO Hoosier peanut butter powder. Add to various recipes to make delicious smoothies, shakes, oatmeals, and yogurts.

The mix has been especially formulated to help you access nutrient dense, plant based energy; rich in protein, fiber, essential amino acids, and minerals like iron, calcium, and magnesium.

We will soon be launching a new product; the Roamers Superseed Mix, it is 100% organic, nut-free, and paleo friendly.
